{{'Step 1/5 - Preamble'|translate}}

Please read carefully.

You are about to register your decentralized application (dapp) on the PLTChainCoin network. After the registration all PLTChainCoin users will be able to see your dapp on the PLTCHAIN Dapp Store and they will also be able to install and launch it.

One of the characteristics of blockchains is that you can't delete any content from them. Therefore make sure you have used the correct dapp parameters. We also advise you to test your dapp on the official PLTCHAIN testnet so you can guarantee that its working flawlessly.

Things you need to know for the registration process:

• Dapp names are unique, you can't register the same name twice on the PLTCHAIN network.
• You can either use a centralized (GitHub) or decentralized (Sia) hosting method.
• Make sure that your dapp icon's format is a square.
• Choose your tags wisely, with them your users will be able to find your dapp in the Dapp Store.

The PLTCHAIN Team isn't liable for a dapp, solely the uploader is. So you have to make sure that your dapp is legal and doesn't harm its users.
